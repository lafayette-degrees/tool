Who am I?

I am a proud Eagle Scout and have been since 2023. I currently go to school at a Polytechnic Institute studying computer science with a concentration in cyber-security. Outside of computers however, I really enjoy hiking and camping (wilderness survival in general), as well as biking, and started going to the gym frequently since last Summer. Music is a big passion of mine, and some artists/bands I've been listening to a lot recently (at time of writing) are TOOL, BONES, Gojira, Deftones, and Ye West (mainly his older stuff :/).

Mainly so that I have a place on the internet that is 100% mine, to develop web-development skills, and to share my ideas & projects with others. I think everyone needs some sort of outlet to express themselves. Do I think my articles/posts/projects contain world changing ideas? Probably not. But that won't stop me from trying.
