Photography

I like to take pictures just as a fun side hobby. The best way to do it isn't with a phone or a several thousand dollar camera though. It's with a old camera from the mid 2000s. Why? Well it's cheap, easy, and the older technology comes with a couple neat quirks and artifacts. Some may not like it, but I love the style. I take all these photos by myself. I do not receive help with positioning, angles, or even just having someone else hold the camera. It forces you to get a bit creative! Again, these photos have been optimized for the web, so they are much lower quality than I would like, but it is what it is. These aren't all the good photos I've taken obviously, but just a few that stood out to me.

Gifs

As I described earlier in the photography section, I use a pretty old camera. The video function on it works, well, not that great. But honeslty, it has such a cool look to it. The gritty old details, the inaccurate colors, and the terrible resolution all comes together to create something beautiful. Please enjoy a few videos I cut down into replayable gifs!
