about me

instagram

I began making music back in 2020 using an online DAW called Soundtrap. It was great for beginners, but I quickly felt its limits. A few months later, I moved on to using FLStudio 20, which is still my current DAW. I have thought about changing again, but I am unsure as I do not produce music nearly as much as I used to nowadays. Still, I made countless EPs and one final debut album. The EPs have since been archived due to their early nature. The album however is still up on my bandcamp.

As I make new music, it will likely appear on my Bandcamp or this page!
